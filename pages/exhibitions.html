Exhibitions

Taking our artworks to exhibitions has been a wonderful way to share our pieces with the public as well as other artists. Many artists within Whangarei have had similar ideas surrounding the restoration and recycling of old materials. This has led to much inspiration and growth for us. Our last exhibition was the ArtBeat 2023 Exhibition, an amazing event that we were so proud to be a part of.
The next exhibition we are in is coming soon - Riversdale Mixed media Exhibition. We are so pleased to be invited to this exhibition, our work has been reserved and is in the process of being displayed Images from the exhibition will be added to the website and all of our social medias so make sure you follow us to find out more information!
